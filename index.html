Our Story

A Tradition of Pure
Vegetarian Excellence

House of Venu Krishna was born from a simple vision — to bring the finest vegetarian cuisine to the heart of Chennai, creating unforgettable dining experiences for families.

For years, House of Venu Krishna has been delighting customers with authentic vegetarian cuisine inspired by traditional South Indian flavors. Every meal is prepared using fresh ingredients, time-honored recipes, and a passion for quality. What started as a small eatery has blossomed into one of Sowcarpet's most beloved vegetarian dining destinations.
